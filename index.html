About Foodnow

FoodNow is an online ordering and food delivery service located in 20 cities across Indonesia. We make ordering food simple, fun, and accessible via desktop or mobile devices.

Our focus goes beyond food, partnering with local businesses, schools, groups, and restaurants, bringing our customers the best experience possible.

How it's Works

Using FoodNow is as easy as 1..2..3..

  1. 1Select a restaurant or type of food from the available categories and recommendations that GO-FOOD has personalised only for you.
  2. 2Or enter the name of a restaurant or type of food would you like.
  3. 3Select the type and quality of dish(es) you would like to order.
  4. 4Enter the delivery address and select the payment method, then click on Order.
  5. 5Your order is on it's way!
